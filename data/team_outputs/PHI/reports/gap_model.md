## Interpretation

- These are not generic player-quality categories.
- A candidate only gets need-match credit when later skill-profile evidence allows the matching claim.
- Center gaps do not open the starting-center slot because Embiid is a locked core player.
- Missing injury, shot-type, and private availability data lower confidence instead of being invented.
