## Limitations

- Cohorts use public team and player-season proxies.
- Historical player-season positions are filled from current NBA.com bio positions when missing, so role counts are useful proxies rather than exact historical lineup classifications.
- Lineup/on-off and possession-level context are not fully sourced.
- Blueprint distance is descriptive, not a prediction of playoff success.
