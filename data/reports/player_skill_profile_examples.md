## Limits

- Movement shooting, rim pressure, vertical spacing, fouls, and transition pressure need richer tracking data.
- Defense claims require more than steals: position, size, minutes, and defensive-event evidence are all checked.
- These profiles allow or block claims. They do not replace scouting.
